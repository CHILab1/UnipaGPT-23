Il corso di INTERNATIONAL SOCIAL WORK:GLOBAL ISSUES IN MIGRATIONS AND DEVELOPMENT (in inglese) del corso di Laurea magistrale in COOPERAZIONE, SVILUPPO E MIGRAZIONI , ha i seguenti obiettivi formativi: 
The course intends to provide the basic knowledge of the study and research  objects of the Sociology of Migration. Some of the themes on this course will  cover the origins of sociology of migrations, sociological paradigms, as well as  the many study objects created by the various migratory phenomena and the  responses of different European countries. The study will include an exploration  of the complexity of migration factors in countries of origin, the elements  involved in migration choices and the plurality of routes taken to destination  countries.
