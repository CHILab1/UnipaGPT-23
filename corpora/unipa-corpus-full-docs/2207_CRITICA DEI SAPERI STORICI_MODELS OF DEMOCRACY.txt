Il corso di MODELS OF DEMOCRACY del corso di Laurea magistrale in SCIENZE FILOSOFICHE E STORICHE del curriculum CRITICA DEI SAPERI STORICI, ha i seguenti obiettivi formativi: 
The course aims at developing the analytical skills of students in relation to  democratic theory. The course ha a twofold structure. First, it introduces the  student to the use of analytical models of democracy (procedural, aggregative,  participatory, deliberative) proposed in contemporary debates. Second, it  pushes the student to employ those analytical models to analyse and assess the  democratic content of recent political and institutional change.
