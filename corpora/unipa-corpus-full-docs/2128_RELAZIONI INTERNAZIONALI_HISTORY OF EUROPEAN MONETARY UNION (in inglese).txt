Il corso di HISTORY OF EUROPEAN MONETARY UNION (in inglese) del corso di Laurea in SCIENZE POLITICHE E DELLE RELAZIONI INTERNAZIONALI del curriculum RELAZIONI INTERNAZIONALI, ha i seguenti obiettivi formativi: 
The main objectives of this course regard the study of the process leading to the  European monetary union, with a focus on: 1. its theoretical pre requisites, 2. its  expected macroeconomic and microeconomic benefits/costs, 3. its main  institutional and policy changes, 4. its achievements, failures and main  obstacles.
