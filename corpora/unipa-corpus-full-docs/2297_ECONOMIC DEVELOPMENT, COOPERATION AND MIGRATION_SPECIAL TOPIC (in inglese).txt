Il corso di SPECIAL TOPIC (in inglese) del corso di Laurea in ECONOMIA E COOPERAZIONE INTERNAZIONALE PER LO SVILUPPO SOSTENIBILE del curriculum ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, COOPERATION AND MIGRATION, ha i seguenti obiettivi formativi: 
The class gives students the possibility to analyze migration phenomenon from  a practitioner perspective and to provide students with specialized linguistic  skills and knowledge of selected issues and the most advanced methods of  analysis. The class will help students develop practical knowledge and acquire  English terminology.
