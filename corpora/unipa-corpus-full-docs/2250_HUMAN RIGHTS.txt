Il corso di HUMAN RIGHTS del corso di Laurea magistrale in RELIGIONI E CULTURE , ha i seguenti obiettivi formativi: 
Educational Objectives The goal of the course is to give students critical tools to know, understand and  put in practice an approach useful to cope with consequences deriving by the  nexus working between human vulnerability reduction policies, building  resilience interventions, risk reduction strategies and the Neoliberal  Governmentality, in sustainable development and humanitarian real contexts.
